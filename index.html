Lighting

Button assignments

Reassign each physical button. All buttons are written together when you apply.

Turbo / rapid-fire

Bind a button to fire a click repeatedly. Count 0 fires continuously while the button is held. The mouse sets the cadence; there's no per-button interval byte in this firmware.

Macro recorder

Record a sequence of key & mouse presses, tweak the timing, then bind it to a button.
Click here, then press keys and mouse buttons to record them.

Polling rate

How often the mouse reports its position to the PC. Higher = smoother, more battery use. Wired and wireless rates are stored separately.

Lift-off & motion

Lift-off is the height at which the sensor stops tracking when you raise the mouse.
